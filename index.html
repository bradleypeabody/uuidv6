UUIDs: A Common Case

Universally Unique Identifiers are useful in many scenarios. And RFC 4122 describes 5 versions, indicated for use in various cases.

This document describes a proposed sixth version which in the author's opinion addresses a relatively minor but nonethless significant shortcoming in the UUID specification, specifically when compared to the Version 1 UUIDs from the RFC.
